# Didoo AI - AI Agent for Meta Ads Automation

## What is Didoo AI?
Didoo AI is a no-code AI Agent platform built specifically for small and medium businesses to automate Meta ads creation, audience targeting, and campaign optimization. With just a single URL input, the platform generates complete, ready-to-launch Meta advertising campaigns in under one minute—eliminating the need for agencies, design tools, or manual A/B testing.

## Use Cases
- **E-commerce Product Launches**: Convert Amazon, Shopify, or any product URL into live Meta campaigns with auto-generated creatives and targeting
- **SaaS App Install Campaigns**: Drive user acquisition with AI-optimized hooks and micro-audience segmentation for software products
- **Local Business Promotion**: Transform basic business URLs into geo-targeted ads that reach high-intent local customers
- **Multi-Client Agency Management**: Scale campaigns across 7+ client accounts simultaneously with real-time performance dashboards
- **Subscription Box Growth**: Identify profitable micro-niches and optimize ROAS for recurring revenue models
- **Content Creator Monetization**: Auto-generate conversion-focused ads for online courses, digital products, and affiliate offers
