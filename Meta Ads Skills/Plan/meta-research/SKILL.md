# Meta Ads Market Research

## When to Use
Before launching a new campaign or entering a new market — to understand the competitive landscape, what types of ads are working, and where opportunities exist.

---

## Step 1: Define Research Scope
Ask:
- What industry or category? (e.g., SaaS, e-commerce, local services)
- What markets or countries?
- What product or service type?
- Any specific competitors to analyze?

---

## Step 2: Competitive Ad Intelligence
Use Meta's Ad Library (ads.facebook.com/ads-library) and web search to find:

**What competitors are running:**
- What hooks are they using?
- What offers are they making?
- What creative formats (video, carousel, single image)?
- What's their messaging angle?

**How to find them:**
1. Search Meta Ad Library for competitor brand names
2. Search for "[industry] ads" on web search to find articles/case studies
3. Use web fetch to pull competitor landing pages

**What to document for each competitor:**
- Brand name
- Primary hook/offer
- CTA used
- Estimated ad spend signal (from Ad Library if available)
- Creative format
- Landing page URL

---

## Step 3: Market Trend Research
Use web search to find:

**Industry benchmarks:**
- What's the average CPM for this industry/market?
- What's the average CPL or CPA?
- Any seasonal trends to know about?

**What's working now:**
- Recent case studies in this category
- New creative formats or angles gaining traction
- Platform changes or new Meta features to leverage

---

## Step 4: Gap and Opportunity Analysis
Based on what you find, identify:
- White space: Angles or formats competitors aren't using
- Overtime: Where competitors are weakest (high CPL, weak creative)
- Differentiation opportunity: What unique angle could the brand use?

---

## Step 5: Competitor Landing Page Evaluation
Evaluate the landing pages competitors are sending traffic to — this tells you more about their conversion capability than the ads themselves.

| Element | What to check | Red flag |
|---------|---------------|----------|
| Load speed | Does the page render quickly on mobile? | > 3 second load |
| CTA clarity | Is there a clear action above the fold? | No CTA visible without scrolling |
| Form friction (lead gen) | How many form fields? | More than 4 fields |
| Trust signals | Logos, reviews, guarantees? | None visible |
| Mobile-first | Is it designed for mobile or desktop? | Desktop-only layout |
| Offer clarity | Is the offer immediately clear? | Vague or buried messaging |

---

## Key Research Sources
- Meta Ad Library: ads.facebook.com/ads-library — search by brand or category
- Google Search: "[industry] Meta Ads case study", "[competitor] ads"
- Web Fetch: Pull competitor landing pages to understand their full funnel
- SimilarWeb / SpyFu: Competitive traffic and ad spend estimates

---

## Restrictions
- Do not make up data — only report what you actually find
- Do not use competitor branding or trademarks in ways that violate Meta policies
- Present findings objectively — don't bias toward a predetermined conclusion
